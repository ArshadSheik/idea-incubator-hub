About Idea Incubator Hub

A student-built platform for CITS3403/5505 at UWA — share early-stage ideas, get community feedback, and find people to build with.

What is it?

Idea Incubator Hub is a community platform where anyone can post a startup idea, receive upvotes and structured feedback from other users, and find collaborators to actually build it with.

Ideas move through stages — from Ideation through Validation and Building all the way to Launched. At each stage the community can vote, comment, and jump on board as collaborators.
