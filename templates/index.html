🛠️ How it works

Four steps from blank page to launched product.

01

Drop your idea

Write it down while it's fresh. Title, description, why-it-matters, category. That's it.

02

Let the community vote

Votes, comments, and critiques roll in. You'll know within days if it's got legs.

03

Assemble your team

Invite collaborators who match your skill gaps — designers, devs, marketers, domain experts.

04

Build in public

Track progress on your Kanban board. Share milestones. Launch to the whole community.
